
the italian sportscar manufacturer presents a limited special edition of its successful sportscar. the lamborghini gallardo se (special edition) which is limited to 250 units is characterized by a two-tone body paint, an even more sporty interior and technical improvement. the basis for the special edition is the extreme, uncompromising and unsurpassed lamborghini gallardo.
the gearbox is now equipped with shorter gear ratios in the first five gears, enhancing the dynamic performance of the vehicle. the result is also shown in the acceleration from 0-100km/h in 4.0 seconds which improved by 0.2 seconds, achieving a top speed of 315 km/h versus 309 km/h previously. this is shown impressively on an e.gear equipped car with a feature called “thrust mode”, optimizing acceleration and gear change from a standing start throughout all gears.
source: lamborghini press release


engine: 5.0l v10
weight: 1,430 kg / 3,153 lbs
power: 382 kw / 512 hp / 520 ps
0-62 mph: 3.9 seconds
torque: 510 nm / 376 lb-ft
top speed: 315 kph / 195 mph
