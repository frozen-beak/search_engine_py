
2025 porsche 911 gt3 revealed, with the 992.2 generation keeping the high revving naturally aspirated flat 6 and manual gearbox. with emission laws getting tighter year on year, porsche had to work hard to keep the 911 gt3 over the desired 500 hp mark whilst also keeping it in its pure na form. they have fitted the camshafts from the gt3 rs, redesigned the cylinder head and optimised the flow of the individual throttle bodies. the end result is peak power of 503 hp and 340 lb-ft of torque.


engine: 4.0l flat 6
weight: 1,420 kg / 3,130 lbs
power: 375 kw / 503 hp / 510 ps
0-62 mph: 3.4 seconds
torque: 460 nm / 340 lb-ft
top speed: 310 kph / 193 mph
