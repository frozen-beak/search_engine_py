
porsche have updated their flagship macan with the gts getting more power and revised styling. with this mid-life refresh porsche have dropped the turbo model macan and given the gts a good power bump to help compensate. power has risen by 58 hp to a new high of 434 hp, which is the same as the outgoing turbo macan. the 0-62mph acceleration has improved with porsche now claiming a time of 4.3 seconds and a top speed of 168 mph.
other changes for the revised macan gts includes air suspension with stiffer springs and a 10mm lower ride height. adaptive dampers are now standard, and the optional gts sports package adds a torque-vectoring rear differential, 21-inch wheels and the sports chrono package.
