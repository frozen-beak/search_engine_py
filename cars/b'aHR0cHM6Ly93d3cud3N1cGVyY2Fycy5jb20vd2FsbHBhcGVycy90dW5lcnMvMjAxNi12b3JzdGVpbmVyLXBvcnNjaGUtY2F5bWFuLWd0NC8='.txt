
vorsteiner have revealed their new aero kit specifically made for the porsche cayman gt4. made from autoclaved pre-impregnated carbon fiber, vorsteiner claim their production method provides superior stiffness, aerospace strength & durability compared to traditional hand laid carbon fiber composites.
the parts available for the cayman gt4 include a front splitter, side skirts, riser extensions, hood, rear diffuser and rear wing with end caps. vorsteiner also have a series of custom forged wheels that feature on the gt4.
