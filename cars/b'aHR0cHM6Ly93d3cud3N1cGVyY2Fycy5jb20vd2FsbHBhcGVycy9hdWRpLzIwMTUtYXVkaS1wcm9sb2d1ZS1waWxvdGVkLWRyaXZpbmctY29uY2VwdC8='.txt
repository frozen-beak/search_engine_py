
among the highlights of audi’s 2015 ces presence is the hybrid-powered audi prologue piloted driving showcar which was purpose-built for ces.
the audi prologue piloted driving showcar expresses progressive technology in its entire character. its brightly lit interior fuses the car’s architecture with the display and control concept to create a novel unit. the entire front of the instrument is executed as a three-part touch display. add to this an innovative, ultrathin and flexible oled (organic light-emitting diode) display.
the showcar’s hybrid powertrain has a muscular 4.0 tfsi working together with a powerful e-motor. system power output of 505 kw / 677 hp) and a combined torque of both engines of 950 nm / 700.7 lb-ft) enable the audi prologue piloted driving to accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h (62.1 mph) in 3.5 seconds. its co2 emissions are 185 g/km (297.7 g/mi).
source: audi press release
