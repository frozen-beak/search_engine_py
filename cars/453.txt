
kalmar automotive has unveiled the 9x9, its modern-day 917 hp take on the iconic porsche 959. almost four decades after the porsche 959 was unveiled at the frankfurt motor show with cutting-edge tech, all-wheel drive and a top speed record, kalmar have shown off their new interpretation. developed over three years, the 9x9 will be available in three different specs, standard, sport and leichtbau (lightweight), with nine examples of each set to be built.
