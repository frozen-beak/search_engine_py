
unveiled at the 2009 frankfurt motor show, the lamborghini reventon roadster was the follow up to the popular reventon from 2008. the mechanicals were used from the murcielago lp640 super veloce, and only 15 unit were made. the styling was very angular and aggressive, previewing future design choices for the company with the aventador and huracan featuring similar styles.
“the reventón is the most extreme car in the history of the brand,” comments stephan winkelmann, president and ceo of automobili lamborghini s.p.a. “the new roadster adds an extra emotional component to our combined technological expertise – it unites superior performance with the sensual fascination of open-top driving.”


engine: 6.5l v12
weight: 1,690 kg / 3,726 lbs
power: 471 kw / 631 hp / 640 ps
0-62 mph: 3.4 seconds
torque: 660 nm / 487 lb-ft
top speed: 330 kph / 205 mph
