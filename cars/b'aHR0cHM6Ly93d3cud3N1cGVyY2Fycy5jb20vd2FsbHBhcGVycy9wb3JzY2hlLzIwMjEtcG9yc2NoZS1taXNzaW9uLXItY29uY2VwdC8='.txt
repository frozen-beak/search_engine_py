
porsche’s all-electric racing car concept the mission r, previews new racer and possible cayman successor. porsche states the main goal of the mission r concept is the creation of a new one-make racing series that would operate in a similar fashion to the 911 carrera cup. perhaps the more interesting aspect to this is the all-electric replacement of the 718 cayman and boxster due in the near future, is this a glimpse into those future models.
running on a 900 volt electrical system with an 80 kwh battery and two motors, the mission r outputs a constant 670 hp. a special qualifying mode will tap into the full potential the powertrain unleashing 1,072 hp, allowing a 0-62 mph acceleration time of 2.5 seconds and a top speed of 186 mph.
