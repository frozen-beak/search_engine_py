
toyota are sending off the a90 generation supra with the most potent version yet. that version is the toyota supra final edition (pictured in black), it come with 429 hp and 420 lb-ft which is a significant lift of both power and torque over the regular turbo inline 6 model. it will also be offered exclusively with the six-speed manual transmission and feature an optimised active rear differential and kw damping-force-adjustable suspension.
available alongside the final edition is the supra lightweight evo (pictured in red), a version that keeps the regular power level but includes some of the improvements of the final edition. the lightweight evo will slot in as the cheaper version of the two.
