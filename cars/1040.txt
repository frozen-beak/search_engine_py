
another year, another bmw vision concept, this time with the look into the future of the bmw m sports car. called the vision m next, this mid-engined plug-in hybrid shares some similarities with the current bmw i8 and iconic bmw m1 from 1979. capable of running in both electric all-wheel drive and rear-wheel drive modes, the vision m next makes use of a mid-ship turbocharged 4 cylinder engine assisted by an electric motor at the front. performance is promised to be strong, with a 0-62 mph time of 3 seconds and a 186 mph top speed.
