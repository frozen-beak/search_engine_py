
audi has previewed the wagon version of it’s new electric-only a6, a car that is well on it’s way to production. both a single and dual motor setup will be offered with the more powerful dual motor offering 462 hp and 590 lb-ft of torque with all-wheel drive. 
the 800-volt electrical system features up to 270 kw fast charging which will be capable of adding 186 miles of range in just 10 mins of charging. going from five to 80% will take roughly 25 minutes, and total range is expected to be around the 434 mile mark from the 100 kwh battery. expect the production a6 e-tron to drop sometime in 2023.
