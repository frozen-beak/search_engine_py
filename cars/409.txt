
porsche are releasing a special 911 gts cabriolet america as a homage to the original 356 america roadster from 1953. being a gts 911, this limited run cabriolet already features a good balance of go fast bits from the 911 catalogue. therefore nothing mechanical has changed with the focus entirely on the red, white and blue aesthetics.
it all starts with the azure blue paint job that has a graphic plastered down the sides of the lower sills featuring the word ‘america’ in white and red. the wheels are white with red striping around the outer edge, the interior has plenty of white and red stitching and the badge are of course white and red.
