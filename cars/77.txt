
lamborghini’s squadra corse racing department have released the evo2 spec of their huracan gt3 racer updated for the 2022 regulations. the update makes use of some styling cues from the huracan sto and includes new safety upgrades in order to meet the new rules. another change is the new roof mounted intake system which feeds air directly to the engine replacing the previous side intake setup. this allows for greater engine response and enables the side intakes to focus on cooling duties.
