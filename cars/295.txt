
audi have injected even more power into the rs6 avant, now with over 620 horsepower from its twin turbocharged v8. with increases of 29 hp and 37 lb-ft of torque, thanks to a rise in boost from 2.4 to 2.6 bar, the new rs6 performance will now accelerate from 0-62 mph in 3.4 seconds. that time is 0.2 seconds quicker, and when optioned with the rs dynamic plus package the top speed is lifted up to 189 mph.


engine: 4.0l v8 twin turbo
weight: 2,090 kg / 4,608 lbs
power: 463 kw / 621 hp / 630 ps
0-62 mph: 3.4 seconds
torque: 850 nm / 627 lb-ft
top speed: 304 kph / 189 mph
