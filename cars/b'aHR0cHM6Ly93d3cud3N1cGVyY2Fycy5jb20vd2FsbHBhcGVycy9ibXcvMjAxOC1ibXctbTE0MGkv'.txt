
the top of the line 1 series, the bmw m140i comes complete with a twin turbo straight 6 engine producing 340 hp and 500 nm of torque. visually the m version gets darker inserts in the front and rear light assemblies, blacked out grille and 18-inch light-weight alloy wheels wrapped in 225/40 tyres at the front, and 245/35 at the rear. other highlights include variable sport steering, m sport suspension, m sport braking system, driving experience control and dynamic stability control system.
the engine setup utilises twin-scroll turbocharging, fully variable valve timing (valvetronic) and double- vanos variable camshaft control. peak torque is available at a low 1,520 rpm and remains strong till 4,500 rpm. transmission options include a six-speed manual that does the 0-100 kph sprint in 4.8 seconds, or an eight-speed steptronic sport transmission that does it in 4.6 seconds. the xdrive m140i will rocket to 100 kph in just 4.4 seconds due to the intelligent all-wheel drive system from bmw.
