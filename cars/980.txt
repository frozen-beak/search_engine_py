
updated bmw m4 cs has arrived with a little more power and sharper handling. the cs’s 3.0l s58 inline-six has gained 20 hp over the m4 competition for a new high of 543 hp. torque remains the same at 479 lb-ft, the 0 to 60 mph sprint will be over in 3.2 seconds, the 0 to 124 mph in 11.1 seconds and the top speed goes up to 188 mph with the m driver’s package. bmw have even taken the new m4 cs to the nürburgring’s nordschleife circuit where it recorded a 7:21.99 lap time around the 12.9-mile track.
