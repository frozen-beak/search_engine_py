
the csl nameplate for bmw only appears once in a while with some great cars donning the badge, now the new m4 csl has arrived with a lot to live to. standing for competition, sport, lightweight, this csl is 100 kilograms lighter, 40 hp more powerful and rear-wheel drive only.
this csl also happens to be the fastest bmw ever with a 0-62 mph sprint of 3.7 seconds, 0-124 mph sprint of 10.7 seconds and a top speed of 190 mph. on fact it’s even clocked in a nürburgring lap time of 7:15.677, the best lap time any bmw has managed around the “green hell”.


engine: 3.0l inline 6 twin turbo
weight: 1,625 kg / 3,583 lbs
power: 405 kw / 543 hp / 550 ps
0-62 mph: 3.7 seconds
torque: 650 nm / 479 lb-ft
top speed: 306 kph / 190 mph
