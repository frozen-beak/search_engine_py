
new lamborghini urus s gets the performante’s 656 hp v8 without the harsh chassis tune. this is the urus for those who aren’t interested in doing track days but still want the extra power and torque of the recently announced performante. power peaks at 656 hp, and torque comes in at 627 lb-ft between 2,300 to 6,000rpm. the 0-62 mph acceleration time has dropped slightly from 3.3 to 3.5 seconds, and the 0-124 mph is a full 1 second slower at 12.5 seconds.
