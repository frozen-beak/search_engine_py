
mercedes new eqe has been revealed, with the range topping amg 53 version packing 677 horsepower. there will be two amg eqe’s with the 43 and 53 models offering a performance based take on the all new electric eqe. as is expected with the “e-class” cars from mercedes, the eqe is slightly smaller in size to the eqs, but retains a similar deign and shape.
the highest spec 53 model comes with 677 hp and 738 lb-ft of torque, and will do 0-62 mph in 3.3 seconds. the 43 model produces 476 hp with 633 lb-ft and does 4.2 seconds. both have a dual motor setup with one located on each axle providing all-wheel drive and using a single speed transmission.
