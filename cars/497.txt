
porsche is now celebrating 75 years since their first production model the 365 with a 357 speedster concept car. it follows on from the 357 concept shown off at the start of 2023 with one key difference, well two. firstly this is a single seater, open-top car with a tonneau cover over the passenger side of the car. secondly, the 4.0l flat 6 engine has been replaced with two electric motors, one positioned on the front axle and the other on the rear.
