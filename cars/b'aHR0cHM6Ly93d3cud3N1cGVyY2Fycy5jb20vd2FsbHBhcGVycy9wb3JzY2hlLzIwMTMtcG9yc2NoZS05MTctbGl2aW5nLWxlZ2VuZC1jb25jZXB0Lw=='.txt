
one of the most famous race cars around is the iconic porsche 917, and the porsche 917 living legend concept is a modern day tribute to the 1970 24 hours of le mans winner. this special concept was developed in just 6 months time in 2013, and is not a complete working car, in fact it appears just be a painted clay model with wheels. that however does not hold it back from being a stunning looking model, which shares some styling elements that would later appear on the mighty 918.
the 917 living legend concept is part of several concept cars porsche have revealed to the public in november 2020, lifting the lid on a secretive part of the automotive world where designers set out to explore ideas that ultimately are kept behind closed doors.
