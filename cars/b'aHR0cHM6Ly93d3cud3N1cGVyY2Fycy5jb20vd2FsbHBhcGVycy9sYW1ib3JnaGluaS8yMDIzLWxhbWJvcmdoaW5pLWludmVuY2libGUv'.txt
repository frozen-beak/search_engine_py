
lamborghini’s ultimate expression of the mighty v12, the invencible will be the last naturally aspirated v12 lambo built. the styling takes elements from previous lamborghini v12 supercars like the engine cover, exhaust and intakes from the sesto elemento. pumping out 769 hp and 531 lb-ft of torque, the invencible has nothing less than the most potent tune of the 6.5l v12 lamborghini offers. a fitting end to its reign of glorious free breathing v12 power.

0-62 mph: 2.8 seconds
