
the seventh-generation of bmw’s 7-series has just dropped, and it comes with hybrid and a full electric option for the first time. for the gasoline lovers out there bmw still has you covered with the new 7-series, equipping the 760i xdrive with a 536 hp 4.4l twin turbocharged v8 engine.  those looking for a little better fuel economy may prefer the 740i with it’s 3.0l turbocharged inline 6 and 48-volt mild-hybrid system. then there’s the i7, the first full electric 7-series produced by bmw that has an epa-rated 300+ mile range and the same power output as the v8 model.
