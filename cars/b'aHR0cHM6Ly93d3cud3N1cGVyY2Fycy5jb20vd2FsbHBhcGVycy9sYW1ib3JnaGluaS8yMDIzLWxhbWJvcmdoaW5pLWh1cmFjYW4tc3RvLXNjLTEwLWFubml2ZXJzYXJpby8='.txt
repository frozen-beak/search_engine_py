
this is the most extreme road-legal lamborghini huracan to date, and it’s only being made for one lucky customer. commissioned through the brands ad personam program, the huracan sto sc 10 anniversario is very close to being a road-legal super trofeo race car and is made to celebrate the near decade long production run of the huracan. everything has been sharpened up with the chassis tuned to be harder and stiffer and the aero setup to generate even more downforce than before. lamborghini’s squadra corse race engineers helped to develop the upgrades furthering its race car credentials.
