
nissan’s new z car now has a nismo version, but it’s automatic only and carrying more weight. nissan motorsports have given the z a going over adding a bit more power and torque, 420 and 426 respectively, subtle styling changes and a track day focus. 
the choice of the nine-speed automatic transmission comes as nissan claim their buyers want faster lap times, and that this is how they are achieving that. the 0-60 mph time in the auto is 0.6 seconds quicker than a manual would be with nissan revising the clutch packs with extra plates for quicker shifting.
