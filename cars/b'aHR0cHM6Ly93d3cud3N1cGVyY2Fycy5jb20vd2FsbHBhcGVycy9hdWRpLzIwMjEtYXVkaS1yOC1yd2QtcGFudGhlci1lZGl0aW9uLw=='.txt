
exclusively for the us market is the audi r8 rwd panther edition, which feature exclusive interior and exterior design elements. with numbers limited to only 30 units in total and all in coupe form, the panther edition r8 will launch the r8 rear-wheel-drive model line as the first available.
the exclusive features list includes a special bodywork paint finish called panther black that has a crystal effect to make it extra special. big 20 inch wheels with a bespoke design that are milled cut and finished in matte black with red trim matches the red accents in the interior. and a sports exhaust has been fitted with adjustable valving to help amplify the v10 engine.
