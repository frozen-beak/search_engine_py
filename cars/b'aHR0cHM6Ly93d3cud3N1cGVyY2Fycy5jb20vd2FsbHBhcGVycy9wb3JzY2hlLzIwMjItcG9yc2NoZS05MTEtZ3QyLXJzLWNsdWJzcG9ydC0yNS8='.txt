
celebrating manthey-racing’s 25th anniversary, porsche motorsport have released the track-only 911 gt2 rs clubsport 25. based on the clubsport model from 2019, porsche have sprinkled in some goodies from the 935 and limited production to just 30 units.
the engine and transmission remain the same as the standard gt2 clubsport, with 691 hp and 553 lb-ft of torque on tap and a seven-speed pdk transmission. where the magic begins is with the wider track, completely redesigned suspension, revised aerodynamic bodywork, uprated electronic control systems and steering gear.
