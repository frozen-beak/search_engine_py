
united states tuning company ss customs have given the pagani huayra supercar a new custom color with a chrome wrap. the colour is a satin black chrome with red reflective pinstriping to highlight the beautifully crafted lines of huayra. completing the look are naked carbon fiber parts that provide a textured contrast to the glossy wrapped panels. to finish it all off, the wheels are finished in black.


engine: 6.0l v12 twin turbo
weight: 1,440 kg / 3,175 lbs
power: 537 kw / 720 hp / 730 ps
0-62 mph: 3.3 seconds
torque: 1,000 nm / 738 lb-ft
top speed: 374 kph / 232 mph
