
world champions for the last 4 straight years, mercedes-amg are back with their w09 eq power+, ready to aim at championship number 5. though their main rivals in ferrari and red bull racing have made inroads over the last 1 to 2 years, mercedes has kept one step ahead and not put a foot wrong when it counted. the success of the team has been impressive for four years now and doesn’t look to stop any time soon.
the driver line-up stays the same from last season with 4 time world champion lewis hamilton leading the way, and his team mate valtteri bottas looking to become a serious title contender.
