
celebrating the 20th anniversary since the first audi tt was launched, is the competition plus version of the audi tts in both coupe and roadster forms. the new equipment pack includes subtle styling changes to the tt with some fake vents front and rear, revised front fascia with splitter and a fixed rear wing. led lights will now comes standard with the competition plus, as well as red painted brake calipers, 10-spoke 20-inch wheels finished in high gloss black and black exterior badges.
power for the tts has been taken up a notch, with the 2.0l inline 4 turbocharged engine now producing 315 hp with 400 nm of torque. that’s a modest increase of 9 hp and 20 nm from the previous tts. 0-62 mph is quoted at 4.5 seconds with a top speed electronically limited to 155 mph.
