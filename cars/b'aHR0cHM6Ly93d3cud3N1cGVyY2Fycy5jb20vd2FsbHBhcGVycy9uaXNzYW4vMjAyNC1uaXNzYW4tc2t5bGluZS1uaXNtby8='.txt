
nissan unveils their new skyline nismo, fitted with over 400 horsepower but reserved for the japanese market only. sitting atop the skyline family, the nismo leverages nissan motorsport’s expertise to produce a car that’s even faster, more comfortable and safer than its illustrious predecessors.
the vr30ddtt engine has been tuned to raise its peak power and torque outputs to 414 hp and 405 lb-ft respectively. nismo have also tweaked the automatic transmission to deliver a sharper response and higher rpm’s when in sport and sport+ modes. the suspension and chassis have also had a going over helping to keep the skyline stable when pushing the performance edge.
