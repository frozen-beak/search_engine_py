
at the 2021 shanghai auto show audi have released the a6 e-tron concept, previewing the next generation electric-only a6. built on the all-new premium platform electric (ppe) architecture which was co-developed with porsche, the next gen a6 audi will not have any internal combustion engine versions in the line-up.
the 800-volt electrical system features up to 270 kw fast charging which will be capable of adding 186 miles of range in just 10 mins of charging. going from five to 80% will take roughly 25 minutes, and total range is expected to be around the 434 mile mark from the 100 kwh battery.
both a single and dual motor setup will be offered with the more powerful dual motor offering 462 hp and 590 lb-ft of torque with all-wheel drive. expect the production a6 e-tron to drop sometime in 2023.
