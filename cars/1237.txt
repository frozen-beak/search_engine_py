
mercedes-amg has revealed its second-generation gt, combining v8 power with all-wheel drive grip in a coupe body. initially it will be available in two variants, the 55 and the top spec 577 hp 63. with a focus on everyday useability, mercedes have added a 2+2 seating option along with rear-axle steering, variable all-wheel drive for all models and split folding rear seats for extra useability.


engine: 4.0l v8 twin turbo
weight: 1,970 kg / 4,343 lbs
power: 430 kw / 577 hp / 585 ps
0-62 mph: 3.2 seconds
torque: 800 nm / 590 lb-ft
top speed: 315 kph / 196 mph
