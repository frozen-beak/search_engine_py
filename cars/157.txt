
audi are setting their sights on a new lineup of electric vehicles tailored to china through a joint venture with saic. part of this new approach is the removal of the iconic four rings badging on the front and rear of the car. instead, they have been replaced with “audi” badges that both light up on the e concept car. featuring a dual motor setup that is fed by a 100 kwh battery, the e concept has 764 hp and590 lb-ft of torque. it also has an all-wheel drive system and will accelerate from 0 to 62 mph in 3.6 seconds.
