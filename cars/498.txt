
celebrating its 75th anniversary, porsche have created a special one-off 357 concept car paying tribute to the original 356 porsche. it’s a blend of proportions that are reminiscent of the historical porsche archetype, and a visualisation of the outlook for the future of the brand. in other words, they are looking to the future whilst referencing the past. built on the 718 cayman gt4 rs, the 357 concept features a 4.0l flat 6 engine producing 493 hp and 332 lb-ft of torque.
