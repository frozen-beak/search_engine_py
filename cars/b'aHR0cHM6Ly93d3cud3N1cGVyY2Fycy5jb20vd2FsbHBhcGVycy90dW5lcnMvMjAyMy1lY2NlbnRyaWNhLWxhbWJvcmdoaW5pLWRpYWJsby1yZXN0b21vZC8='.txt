
eccentrica cars have brought an iconic supercar of the 1990’s into the 21st century with a lamborghini diablo restomod. based in san marino, eccentrica have revealed a modern take on the diablo with more power and a refreshed design inside and out. 
every exterior body panel has been tweaked to transform the diablo into a supercar that could just about pass as a brand-new creation in 2023. inside, the interior has also been modernised whilst keeping some of the charm of the original.
the lamborghini 5.7l v12 engine has been upgraded with valvetrain modifications and a capristo exhaust system to generate 542 hp and 442 lb-ft of torque. 0-62 mph should be done in 3.5 seconds, and the top speed is rated at 208 mph.
