
chiron sport les legendes du ciel honours the “daredevils” of the last century, bugatti racing drivers who were also skilled pilots. in a throwback to aircrafts from the 1920s, the chiron sport les legendes du ciel has been finished in a special matt-grey “gris serpent” paintwork, completed with a single white stripe that extends from front to rear over the cars centerline. the up-turned front edge of the side skirts have been adorned with the french flag, and big red brake calipers sit behind large gloss black wheels.
mechanically everything remains the same is the chiron sport, which means 1,479 hp and 1,600 nm of torque from the big 8.0l w16 engine with four turbochargers. only 20 units are set to made with production beginning at the end of 2020 and priced at 2.88 million euros each.
