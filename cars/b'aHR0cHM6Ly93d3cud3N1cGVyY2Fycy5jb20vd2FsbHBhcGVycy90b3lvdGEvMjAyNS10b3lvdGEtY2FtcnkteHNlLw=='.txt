
toyota’s camry xse is going hybrid for 2025 and ditching the v6 along the way. the new powertrain comprises of a 2.5l inline 4 cylinder engine which is paired with two electric motors to generate a combined 232 hp. this all replaces the old 3.5l v6 used in the previous camry xse, an engine that was more powerful than the new hybrid setup with just over 300 hp.
