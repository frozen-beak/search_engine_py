
nissan’s new heritage edition z car pays tribute to the s30 generation datsun 240z with a distinctive colour scheme and decals. first unveiled 55 years ago in 1969, the 240z remains one of the most iconic japanese cars of all time. nissan america is now celebrating that with a special edition z car featuring a revised front bumper design with more rectangular grille openings, orange bodywork with black decals on the hood, roof and trunk, 19-inch wheels and flared wheel arches on each corner.
