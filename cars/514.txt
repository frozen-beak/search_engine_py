
porsche have unveiled a striking new concept car that promises a new mix of ultra-efficient, low-emission hybrid drive technology. emission levels of 70 grams co2 per kilometre and fuel consumption of three litres/100 kilometres sound like the spec sheet for a small city car, yet here we are with porsche spouting those kind of numbers for their next supercar. a supercar that will accelerate from 0-100 kph in under 3.2 seconds and reach a top speed of over 320 kph (198 mph).
sitting in porches current line-up the carrera gt sits at the top, now the company is showing off its future halo car, with the 918 spyder set to replace the outgoing gt. porsche are expecting a nordschleife of nürburgring lap time of less than 7 minutes 30 seconds, which would be the fastest time porsche has ever managed around the circuit.
