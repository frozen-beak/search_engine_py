
historic porsche specialist tuthill have reimagined the le mans 24 hour winning 911 gt1 race car. that car won porsche’s 16th victory at le mans in 1998 as part of the long since passed fia gt1 class. 
known as the gt one, tuthill takes a 993 generation porsche 911 and transforms it over the course of 3,500 hours into what we see here. only 22 units will be built in total, with each buyer able to spec the car to their individual desires.
