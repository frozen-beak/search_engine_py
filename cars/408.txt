
porsches 911 carrera t brings some sporty goodies to the base model 911 carrera. placed between the base 911 carrera and carrera s models, the carrera t has returned for the 992 generation after the previous 991 version in 2018. key to the changes is a little bit of weight loss, more kit as standard and a seven-speed manual transmission.


engine: 3.0l flat 6 twin turbo
weight: 1,470 kg / 3,241 lbs
power: 283 kw / 380 hp / 385 ps
0-62 mph: 4.5 seconds
torque: 450 nm / 332 lb-ft
top speed: 291 kph / 180 mph
