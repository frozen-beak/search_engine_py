
lamborghini are sending off their legendary v12 engine in style with two one-off supercars. this one is called the autentica, and it along with its sister car the invencible are the last two customer lamborghini’s to come equipped with a naturally aspirated v12 engine. in the coming weeks, lamborghini will unveil the successor to the aventador and it will be powered by a hybrid powertrain along with all new lambo’s.
