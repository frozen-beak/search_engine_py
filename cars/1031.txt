
bmw have given the best look yet at their upcoming top of the line 8-series, with the release of the 4 door m8 gran coupe at geneva. expect the final production design to look very similar to the concept, with a few tweaks here and there. this would be a very good decision, as the newest addition to bmws line-up is a handsome car that looks very powerful and purposeful in full ‘m’ guise.
“the bmw 8-series will take over as the new flagship model of the bmw line-up and, as such, combines unsurpassed sportiness and elegance,” says adrian van hooydonk, senior vice president bmw group design. “the bmw concept m8 gran coupe offers a look ahead to the most exotic and alluring variant of the new bmw 8-series.”
