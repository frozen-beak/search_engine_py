
bmw’s 5-series has spawned an electric i5 version which rocks 600 horsepower for the m60 spec. utilising bmw’s fifth-generation edrive motors, the regular i5 uses a single 335 hp, 243 lb-ft electric motor positioned on the rear axle. for the m60 an extra 257 hp motor is added into the mix on the front axle, with both combining for a total system output of 593 hp and 604 lb-ft of torque. acceleration clocks in at 2.8 second for the 0 to 62 mph and the 81.2 kwh battery should provide a range of around 320 miles for the i5 m60.
