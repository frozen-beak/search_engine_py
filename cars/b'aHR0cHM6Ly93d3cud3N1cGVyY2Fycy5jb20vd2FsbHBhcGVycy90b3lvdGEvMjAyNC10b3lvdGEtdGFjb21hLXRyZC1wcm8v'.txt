
all new toyota tacoma arrives with a brand new engine and plenty of goodies. this is the fourth generation of toyotas mid-sized truck, and to mark the occasion the old 2.7l inline 4 and 3.5l v6 engines are gone and replaced with a 2.4l inline 4 turbo called the i-force. for the trd pro model, toyota have opted for the highest output i-force engine which is called the max, it uses a hybrid setup and delivers peak output of 326 hp and 465 lb-ft of torque whilst paired with an eight-speed auto.
