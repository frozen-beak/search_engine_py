
nissan’s new z car will go racing in japan’s super gt series for 2022, replacing the ageing gt-r. after 14 seasons of competition and 41 race wins in japan’s flagship super gt race series, the gt-r is finally bowing out making way for a new generation racer based on the seventh generation z.
as per gt500 regulations, the 400 hp 3.0l twin turbocharged v6 from the road car has been replaced by a 2.0l direct-injected four-cylinder petrol engine with a single garrett turbocharger. this race prepped engine develops 250 hp more at 650 hp in total which is all sent to the rear wheels through a six-speed sequential transmission.
