
pagani’s newest v12 supercar has lost its roof, this is the utopia roadster. it was nearly two years ago that pagani released the utopia on the world, a manual transmission equipped, twin turbocharged v12 supercar that was the third all-new car in the brands history. the new roadster is the first time the utopia has been offered as a drop top, and removing the roof has not added any additional weight at all. in what is an incredible feat of engineering, pagani have kept the kerb weight exactly the same between both models, tipping the scales at a meagre 2,822 lbs.
