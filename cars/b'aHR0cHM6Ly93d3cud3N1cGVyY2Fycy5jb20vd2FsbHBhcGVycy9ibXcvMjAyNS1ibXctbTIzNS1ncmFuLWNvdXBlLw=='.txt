
bmw’s m235 gran coupe redesign brings a sportier look and modernised interior. the new styling doesn’t stray too far from the original’s but it does bring it more in line with the 5-series. on the engine front, bmw have done some extensive revisions to the turbocharged 2.0l 4 pot. it is said to be more fuel-efficient and have a little more power too, which now tops out at 312 hp. on the downside, peak torque has dropped from 332 to 295 lb-ft.


engine: 2.0l inline 4 turbo
weight: –
power: 233 kw / 312 hp / 316 ps
0-62 mph: 4.9 seconds
torque: 400 nm / 295 lb-ft
top speed: 250 kph / 155 mph
