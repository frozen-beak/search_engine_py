
bugatti is waving good bye to the w16 powertrain as they target hennessey for the fastest convertible crown. before bugatti go all-in on their electric future under rimac, they are sending out the famous quad turbocharged w16 engine with style. the w16 mistral is rocking the most potent tune of that engine, all 1,577 hp of it, the very same as the record setting chiron super sport 300. all that power should give the mistral a fighting chance against the recently revealed hennessey venom f5 roadster as they both vie for the title of world’s fastest convertible.
