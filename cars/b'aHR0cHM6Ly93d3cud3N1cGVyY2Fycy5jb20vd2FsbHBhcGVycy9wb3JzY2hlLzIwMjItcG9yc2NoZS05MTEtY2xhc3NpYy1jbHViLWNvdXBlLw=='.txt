
porsche have revealed an exclusive one-off 996 restoration called the 911 classic club coupe. this is a true one-of-one build by porsche classic and the porsche club of america, that pays homage to the european only 2010 porsche 911 sport classic.
starting life as a 1998 porsche 911 carrera, porsche put the car through extensive works to straighten and strengthen the chassis and fit the engine, chassis and brakes from a 911 gt3 of the same generation. this equates to a naturally aspirated 3.6l flat 6 engine producing 375 hp.
