
built to celebrate 60 years of the 911, porsche have presented a new lightweight special the 911 s/t. designed as a road-biased 911 with the 518 hp engine from the gt3 rs and a manual gearbox, the s/t channels a similar philosophy as the 991-generation 911 r. it forgoes the rs’s eight-speed pdk in favour of a six-speed manual transmission combined with bespoke lightweight drivetrain components.


engine: 4.0l flat 6
weight: 1,380 kg / 3,042 lbs
power: 386 kw / 518 hp / 525 ps
0-62 mph: 3.7 seconds
torque: –
top speed: 300 kph / 186 mph
