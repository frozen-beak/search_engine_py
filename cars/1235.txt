
mercedes two-door amg gt gets the plug-in hybrid treatment, making it the fastest accelerating road-going mercedes ever. positioned as the new flagship model in the gt line-up, the gt63 s e performance combines the usual twin turbo v8 with a rear-mounted electric motor that produces 201 hp on its own. when their powers combine, the gt’s output rises up to 805 hp and 1,047 lb-ft. all of that power and torque is fed through a nine-speed amg speedshift mct transmission and amg’s 4matic+ variable all-wheel drive system, enabling a 0 to 62 mph acceleration time of just 2.8 seconds. 


engine: 4.0l v8 twin turbo hybrid
weight: –
power: 600 kw / 805 hp / 816 ps
0-62 mph: 2.8 seconds
torque: 1,420 nm / 1,047 lb-ft
top speed: 320 kph / 199 mph
