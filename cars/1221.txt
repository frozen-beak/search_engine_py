
the mercedes f1 w05 is a formula one racing car designed by mercedes to compete in the 2014 formula one season. it will be driven by 2008 world drivers’ champion lewis hamilton and nico rosberg, both of whom remain with the team for a second and fifth season, respectively. the f1 w05 has been designed to use mercedes’ new 1.6-litre v6 turbocharged engine, the pu106a hybrid.
