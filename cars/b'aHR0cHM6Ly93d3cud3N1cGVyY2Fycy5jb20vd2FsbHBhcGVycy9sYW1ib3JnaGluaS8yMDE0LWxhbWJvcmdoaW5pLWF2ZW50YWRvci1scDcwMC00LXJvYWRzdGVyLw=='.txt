
the aventador roadster has arrived and it looks like another lambo poster car. rip the roof off one of the most extreme road cars lamborghini has built, and you unleash an even more aggressive look with a wide and low stance that can take it’s place on any bedroom wall poster with ease. the aventador roadster sets the new benchmark for crazy lambos that sound as good as they look.
enjoy the open air while taking off for 0-62 mph in 3.0 seconds, and a top speed maxing out at around 217 mph. the thunderous 6.5l naturally aspirated v12 engine certainly plays it’s part in the theatre of lamborghinis top dog, barking all the right noises through the rev range. peak power has been quoted as 690 hp with 690 nm of torque.


engine: 6.5l v12
weight: 1,624 kg / 3,580 lbs
power: 515 kw / 690 hp / 700 ps
0-62 mph: 3.0 seconds
torque: 690 nm / 509 lb-ft
top speed: 350 kph / 217 mph
