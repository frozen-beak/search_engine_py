
lovers of the wagon rejoice, bmw has finally delivered the m3 touring with all the same bells and whistles as the sedan. launching exclusively in xdrive competition specification, the m3 touring is set to rival the audi rs4 avant in the fast luxury wagon game.
being a competition spec, the m3 touring has all the power and torque of the top line sedan with 503 hp and 479 lb-ft respectively. styling wise the same front end from the m3 sedan has been used which is coupled with a rear end that is like a more sporty/muscular version of the regular 3-series wagon. the split opening tailgate window of the 3-series has remained along with the 500 litres of boot space, which enlarges to 1,510  litres with all the seats folded.
