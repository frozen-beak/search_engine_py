
following on from the murcielago superveloce, the aventador sv has shaved off 50kg and gained 50hp of power over the standard model. 
“the new aventador superveloce continues the lamborghini tradition of sv models, pushing the boundaries in terms of performance and pure driving emotion,” says stephan winkelmann, president and ceo of automobili lamborghini.
“in the superveloce lamborghini’s expertise in lightweight engineering is clearly evident. with technological features which, combined with design, quality standards and craftsmanship, the superveloce results in the most exclusive, pure and emotional production model in the history of our brand.”


engine: 6.5l v12
weight: 1,525 kg / 3,472 lbs
power: 552 kw / 740 hp / 750 ps
0-62 mph: 2.8 seconds
torque: 690 nm / 509 lb-ft
top speed: 350+ kph / 217+ mph
