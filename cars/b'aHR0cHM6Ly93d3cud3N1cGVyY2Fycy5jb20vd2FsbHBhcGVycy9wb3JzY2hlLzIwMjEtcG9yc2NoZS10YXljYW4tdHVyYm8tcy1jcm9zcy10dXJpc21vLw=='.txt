
the taycan range is growing fast as porsche reveal the cross turismo turbo s. taking it’s place at the top of the taycan cross turismo family, the turbo s features the same powertrain as the sedan version of the same spec.
that means two electric motors placed on the front and rear axles combining to generate up to 750 hp in overboost mode or 617 hp normally. acceleration from 0-62 mph in done in a blistering 2.9 seconds which is only 0.1 seconds slower than the sedan version.
