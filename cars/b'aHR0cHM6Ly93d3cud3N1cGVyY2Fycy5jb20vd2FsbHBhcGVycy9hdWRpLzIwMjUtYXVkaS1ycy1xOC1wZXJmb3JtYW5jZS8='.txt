
audi’s rs q8 now has a “performance” model with over 630 horsepower and revised styling. it also comes equipped with carbon-ceramic brakes as standard, optional 23-inch forged wheels, specially tuned air suspension and a boost in peak torque output. the regular rs q8 still remains keeping the same performance level as before.


engine: 4.0l v8 twin turbo
weight: –
power: 471 kw / 632 hp / 640 ps
0-62 mph: 3.6 seconds
torque: 850 nm / 627 lb-ft
top speed: 305 kph / 189 mph
