
spectacular carbon widebody, high-tech forged wheels and supercharged v10 engine rated at 633 kw / 860 hp. n-largo: that is the label under which german car refinement specialist novitec torado presents an especially exclusive super sports car based on the lamborghini huracan. the edition is limited to just 25 cars.
the two-seater impresses visually with a spectacular widebody made from carbon and high-tech forged wheels in a staggered 20/21-inch combination developed specifically for this vehicle. a supercharged version of the 5.2-liter v10 engine rated at around 633 kw / 860 hp is currently under custom-development for the novitec torado n-largo.
